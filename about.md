## About HGGMS

The **Harvard Griffin GSAS Mathematics Society (HGGMS)** is a student-run organization within the Harvard Griffin GSAS. Our mission is to:

1. Build a strong and supportive mathematical community.
2. Facilitate opportunities for collaboration across research groups and interests.
3. Support professional and academic development.
4. Organize events that create space for exploration, discussion, and social connection.

## Activities

HGGMS organizes a wide variety of events, including:

- **Seminars and reading groups**
- **Graduate social events**
- **Workshops and academic support sessions**
- **Interdisciplinary meetups with other GSAS groups**
